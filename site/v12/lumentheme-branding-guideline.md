---

## Rationale

Built from your feedback: sans-serif headlines everyone preferred, Patina's teal that won hearts, and dramatically improved contrast. The Raleway + Lora pairing gives editorial warmth in headlines with rich readability in body text. Product-forward feel. Verdigris CTAs, amber for warmth, signal red only when something's wrong.
